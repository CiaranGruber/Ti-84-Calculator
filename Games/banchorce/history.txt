v2.0.0 <> March 2021
    - Updated to 8-bit colour.
    - Increased map size to 16x12 tiles (up from 16x8).
    - Updated map layouts to incorporate larger size.
    - Added map compression.
    - Added HUD around game screen and removed the redundant Inventory screen.
    - Added crushing frames to stones and rocks (now takes 2 strikes to destroy them).
    - Updated save game system to now have 3 slots.
    - Fixed heart level bug (again?)
    - Reduced the size of "Underground Passage" and "Dark Tunnel" to make room for extra dungeon content.
    - Updated existing dungeon layouts.
    - Added 2 extra dungeons and 2 extra bosses.
    - Boss fights no longer take up maps in the world.
    - Game will create an empty save file if there isn't one on the calculator already (and provided there is enough RAM).
    - Updated King Heath boss fight (now charges at player when the player is in front of him, previously the charge was random).
    - Updated Dezemon boss fight (now rolls around for longer to compensate for larger room).
    - Updated Belkath boss fight (reduced the shooting frequency).
    - Updated Anazar boss fight (made movement pattern smoother and slightly less predictable).
    - Updated Banchor boss fight (charges after player for longer and has two shooting variations).
    - Increased maximum Gold capacity to 99990 (previously 50000).
    - Added Difficulty levels (Easy, Normal, Hard, Hell).
    - Added Cesium icon.
    - Added intro lore when starting a new game.
    - Fixed bug with area names for transitional maps.
    - Reduced enemy damage values.
    - Revised boss damage values, including their bullets.
    - Added many more in-game clues about item locations.
    - Added more gold loot chests in dungeons to encourage exploration and reduce grinding.
    - Added small visual clues to fake walls.
    - Smoothed out enemy bullet movement.
    - Enemy bullets are now deflected by the shield instead of despawning.
    - Rewrote how enemy AI is handled to give more flexibility.
    - Updated path finding for walking enemies such as Potato Bugs, Snakes, etc. so that they don't sit idle in a corner.
    - Updated Frog jump pattern to have some variation.
    - Updated Bee movement to have some variation in circle radius and direction.
    - Updated Bat movement to swoop at the player in arc patterns.
    - Updated Tree Monsters to move more irregularly.
    - Can now pause game during boss fights.
    - Added chance for health orbs to drop from enemies to heal the player half a heart.
    - Increased player damage cooldown.


v1.2.1 <> October 2017
    - Fixed the "warp into wall" bug in one of the maps in the Pyramid of Snakes.
    - Can now press [2nd] to finish talking/reading/viewing inventory.
    - Fixed a bug with partial heart level when starting a game.
    - Wells now fully heal all your hearts, with the cost being increased to 150 Gold (up from 100 Gold).
    - Reduced Gold loss upon death to 1/4 (down from 1/2).
    - Player now revives at a nearby Well of Life after death.


v1.2.0 <> October 2017
    - Ported to TI-84+CE.
    - Minor changes to storyline.
    - Small optimisations.
    - Slowed enemy bullets down by half.
    - Map & save data files can be put into Archive to save RAM.
    - Added flashing animation to show bosses taking damage.
    - Can continue to play game after defeating Banchor, instead of the game resetting.
    - Fixed an exploit whilst fighting bosses.
    - Added player death animation.
    - Gameplay now continues after dying instead of quitting.
    - Increased Gold dropped by certain enemies to reduce grinding a little.
    - Included an Overworld map with the game to make it harder to get lost.


v1.1.0 <> May 2009
    - Rebalanced damage taken from enemies and frequency of damage taken.
    - Rebalanced gold aquired from enemies vs. cost of items.
    - Made it easier to move around and fit through 1 tile gaps (player size is now determined as 6x6 with player / wall collision detection).
    - Made 5th boss bullets easier to see.
